Reason 1: Other readings

I have a terrible habit and I do mean terrible of buying books non-stop. And the reasons behind such impulse are extraordinarily random. I might enjoy the title or the image in the front page, maybe the name of the author sounds familiar or the genre interests me. It does not matter, everypart of a book is an invitation. Currently due to Dostoevsky's influence I find myself fascinated by the context in which Russian literature developed through time. So, ~~why not?~~ I am also reading A Cultural History of Russian Literature.
